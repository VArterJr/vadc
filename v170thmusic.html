On December 22, 2013, my dad's church held a 70th birthday party for him. After the party, we decided to jam on some old songs a bit. Most of the songs have my dad, Vince Arter, Sr., on guitar, me (Vince Arter, Jr.) on bass, and Scott Biondo (our cousin) on drums. On some (I think most) Norma Wagner can be heard playing rhythm guitar in the background (for instance Misty), and in later songs (I'm not sure which ones) Scott's dad (Marty Biondo) can be heard on drums. Also, Scott played rhythm guitar on a few songs.

My dad and Scott played together all the time at their church, The Christian Fellowship Church in St. Charles. I've only played with Scott a few times, and it has been AGES since I've played any of these songs. Some came out well and some... well they were still fun!

Realize this was recorded using the FourTrack app on an iPhone 5S. Yeah... just my iPhone 5S sitting on the counter next to us using its standard microphone. Not bad!

Anyway, enjoy... it was a BLAST... I had hoped to do another session, but Dad passed away before we could put another jam session together with family and friends. I miss him so much.

My Hero

My parents inspired music in our lives. As far back as I can remember music was an everyday thing for our family. I still love music, and thank God for the gift of music in my life. My dad was my hero. He's been gone for a few years now and I miss him every day. I really do miss you, Dad. (March 2022)
