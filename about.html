About Vince Arter, Jr.

(And VinceArter.com)


This site is... uhm... something. No, really it is! It's a conglomeration of things. I've had this site since around 1996 (in one form or the other). The site was originally called DaDen.com. Shortly after that, I succumbed to the trend to have my name as my domain. I sold DaDen.com a few years ago. I have many other domains, including arter.us, but VinceArter.com is my main, personal landing page.

In the past, the site has also been a sharing point for students who took classes I taught part-time at St. Louis County Community College. It has also been a point for sharing ideas with my friends and community developers. It has been a place to share pictures and music I write. It has been a place to share code.

Over the years it has come and gone, seen many approaches to its layout and code. You'll see stuff from development ideas and projects I am working on as well as some of my personal study of music, computer science, and Bible study.

I have removed so much stuff (especially the developer forums, other discussion groups, etc.) since there are so many others to choose from these days. I hate too much duplication, so I've removed much of that stuff.

Thanks for stopping by, and look for more content in the coming months!

Vince Arter, Jr. - 16-March-2022 (Original post circa 2007)
